About Me

I'm a computer engineering student at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor. My work focuses on embedded systems, computer vision, and hardware-software integration. I’ve led and contributed to projects involving sensor fusion, real-time data processing, and custom interfaces using microcontrollers and FPGAs. I thrive at the intersection of hardware and software and currently, I’m doing research in human-computer interaction at the Sensing, Intelligence and eXperience (SIX) Lab led by Doctor Junyi Zhu.
